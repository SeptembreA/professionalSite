About Septembre

Septembre is a creative front-end web developer with an eye for design, a way with words and years of experience in the digital space. Through her work as a developer, journalist and community manager she has worked with a number of brands, organizations and publications in a variety of fields including e-commerce, marketing, public relations, fashion, beauty, lifestyle, food, alcohol, government relations and financial relations.

Septembre is well-versed in a number of languages, frameworks and technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Liquid and Bootstrap on CMSs including Wordpress, Salesforce and NationBuilder. She also has experience with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Sketch and Figma.
